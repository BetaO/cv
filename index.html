Career Summary

I am a Doctor of Science in Mathematics and Computer Science from Mohammed First University, Morocco. After a State Engineering degree in Software Quality at the National School of Applied Sciences, I did a thesis in Model Driven Engineering and whose topic was A model driven approach for a leadership test based platform. My research interests at the MATSI Laboratory (Applied Mathematics, Signal Processing and Computer Science) include model driven engineering, data modeling and Artificial intelligence.
